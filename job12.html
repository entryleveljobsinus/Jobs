Job Description

As a WFM Data Entry Associate at Amazon Inc., you will play a crucial role in ensuring accuracy and efficiency in workforce management data entry. You will be responsible for inputting, updating, and maintaining data related to employee schedules, attendance, and labor forecasts in our proprietary systems. This position is ideal for self-motivated individuals looking to enter the workforce with no prior experience required.

Your tasks will involve:

  • Inputting and updating workforce management data in designated systems.
  • Verifying data accuracy and rectifying discrepancies as needed.
  • Generating regular reports for management review.
  • Maintaining confidentiality and security of sensitive information.
  • Collaborating with team members to gather necessary data.
  • Responding to inquiries related to data entry processes.
  • Participating in training and development programs to enhance skills.
